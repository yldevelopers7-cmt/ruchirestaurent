Our Story

Made with love, served with pride

Ruchi Restaurant was born from a simple belief — that the best food comes from a place of warmth and honesty. Owner Ramesh started with a handful of recipes passed down through generations, and a promise to never compromise on quality.

Located in the heart of Tumkur, Bengaluru, we serve authentic Karnataka dishes including crispy dosas, aromatic biryanis, rich curries, and traditional sweets — all prepared fresh daily using locally sourced ingredients.
